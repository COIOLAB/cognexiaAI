# 📖 **CognexiaAI CRM - Customer User Guide**

Welcome to the CognexiaAI Support System! This guide will help you get the most out of our support portal.

---

## 🚀 **Getting Started**

### **Accessing the Portal**

1. Visit the Client Admin Portal: `http://localhost:3002` (or your organization's URL)
2. Login with your credentials
3. Navigate to the Support section from the dashboard

---

## 📝 **Creating a Support Ticket**

### **Step 1: Navigate to Support Center**
- Click on "Support" in the main navigation
- Or visit `/dashboard/support`

### **Step 2: Create New Ticket**
1. Click the **"New Ticket"** button
2. Fill in the required information:
   - **Subject**: Brief description of your issue
   - **Category**: Choose the appropriate category
     - Technical Issue
     - Billing Question
     - Feature Request
     - Bug Report
     - Other
   - **Priority**: Select based on urgency
     - Low: General questions
     - Medium: Standard issues
     - High: Issues affecting your work
     - Urgent: Critical problems
   - **Description**: Provide detailed information

### **Step 3: Submit Ticket**
- Click **"Submit Ticket"**
- You'll receive a confirmation email
- You'll be redirected to the ticket detail page

### **💡 Tips for Better Support**
- Be as specific as possible
- Include steps to reproduce the issue
- Mention any error messages
- Specify browser/device you're using
- Attach screenshots if helpful

---

## 📋 **Managing Your Tickets**

### **Viewing All Tickets**
1. Go to **"My Tickets"** from the Support Center
2. View all your support requests
3. Use filters to find specific tickets:
   - Search by ticket number or subject
   - Filter by status (Open, In Progress, Resolved, etc.)

### **Ticket Statuses**
- **Open**: Ticket just created, awaiting response
- **In Progress**: Our team is working on it
- **Waiting Response**: We're waiting for more information from you
- **Resolved**: Issue has been solved
- **Closed**: Ticket is complete

### **Priority Levels**
- 🔴 **Urgent**: Critical issue, needs immediate attention
- 🟠 **High**: Affects your work, high priority
- 🔵 **Medium**: Standard issue, normal priority
- ⚪ **Low**: General question, low priority

---

## 💬 **Communicating with Support**

### **Viewing Ticket Details**
1. Click on any ticket from your list
2. See the full conversation history
3. View ticket details in the sidebar

### **Adding Replies**
1. Open the ticket detail page
2. Scroll to "Add Reply" section
3. Type your message
4. Click **"Send Message"**
5. You'll receive an email when support replies

### **What You Can't See**
- Internal notes (only visible to support staff)
- Staff discussions
- System notes

---

## ⭐ **Rating Your Experience**

### **When Can You Rate?**
- After your ticket is marked as "Resolved" or "Closed"
- You'll see a "Rate Your Experience" section

### **How to Rate**
1. Open the resolved ticket
2. Find the rating section
3. Click stars (1-5):
   - ⭐ = Poor
   - ⭐⭐ = Fair
   - ⭐⭐⭐ = Good
   - ⭐⭐⭐⭐ = Very Good
   - ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ = Excellent
4. Your feedback helps us improve!

## ❓ **Frequently Asked Questions**

### **How do I create a ticket?**
Click "New Ticket" from the Support Center, fill in the form, and submit.

### **Can I reopen a closed ticket?**
No, but you can create a new ticket referencing the old one.

### **How do I add attachments?**
You can add attachments when creating a ticket or in replies.

### **Can I update ticket priority?**
No, but you can request a priority change in a message.

### **What if I don't get a response?**
- Check your spam folder
- Verify your email address
- Contact us directly if urgent

### **Can I delete a ticket?**
No, but you can ask support to close it if resolved.
