## Executive Summary

The CRM module will be enhanced from a traditional customer management system to an advanced Industry 5.0 customer intelligence and engagement platform featuring AI-powered customer insights, quantum-enhanced personalization, immersive AR/VR sales experiences, and autonomous customer relationship orchestration.

- **Immersive Sales Experiences:** AR/VR showrooms, virtual meetings, and metaverse engagement
- **Autonomous CRM Orchestration:** Self-managing customer lifecycle and sales processes
- **Predictive Customer Analytics:** Real-time churn prediction and lifetime value optimization
- **Conversational AI:** Advanced chatbots and virtual sales assistants
